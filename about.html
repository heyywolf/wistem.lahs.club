About Us

Our mission is to encourage and support a growing community of women in STEM

Join Us!

The gender gap in STEM is one that impacts not just by professionals in that career but also students at a young age. Seeking to cultivate new mindsets surrounding this issue, we strive to encourage and support a growing community of women in STEM to inspire. To do this, we have two initiatives: reaching back and looking forward.

Reaching Back

Reaching back to the younger students in our community through stem workshops.

Learn more

Looking Forward

Looking forward to the future of us (high school students) through STEM opportunities
