Stempower

STEMpower is a volunteer organization based in the Bay Area and works with WiSTEM, a club focused on empowering students in high school to pursue STEM. Hosting a series of workshops for the younger generation, elementary school students are able to gain exposure to STEM through innovation and creativity. Hosting a series of workshops for the younger generation, elementary school students are able to gain exposure to STEM through innovation and creativity. Through hands-on exploration and team projects, students will have the opportunity to explore topics ranging from water density experiments to Python coding.

STEMpower aims to inspire the next generation of creative thinkers to be interested in STEM fields through hands-on and engaging workshops as part of WiSTEM's giving back initiative. By fostering openness and confidence, students strengthen teamwork, leadership, and communication skills. We seek to create an accepting and empowering learning environment for students as they explore science, technology, engineering, math, and everything in between.
